what is an ERP system

ERP stands for Enterprise Resource Planning. it's basically one big software system that tries to connect all parts of a company — instead of having separate tools for purchasing, inventory, production, HR, and finance, an ERP puts it all in one place with one shared database.

📦 inventory

Tracks parts on hand, bin locations, and stock movements in and out

🛒 purchasing

Manages purchase orders from creation through receipt and payment

🏭 production

Work orders, routing, labor tracking, and shop floor scheduling

✅ quality

Nonconformance reports, discrepancies, scrap tracking
