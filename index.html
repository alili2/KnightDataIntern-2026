🚀 the mission

You're building a business case for whether Knight Aerospace should stay on our current ERP system (Cetec) or switch to something better. That means talking to departments, auditing data quality, researching alternatives, and presenting a real recommendation to leadership. It's a big deal — and it's yours.

one rule we live by: we don't tell leadership "no, it can't be done." we tell them "here's the problem, and here's what we're doing about it." always come with a next step.
